Patient Learning Center

Office of e-Health Innovation and Policy is responsible for the creation and maintenance of UW Health's public and private Web sites.

e-Health consists of a technical team, which manages the programming of the sites and various applications, and an editorial team, which handles the writing and content development.

Health Facts For You

UW Health has one of the best and busiest social media platforms in the United States.

According to MHADegree.org, an online resource for current and aspiring health care administrators, UW Health ranked 9th in their list of the 50 most social media friendly nationally ranked hospitals.
